I am Chunggi Lee, a Research Engineer at Lunit . My work is to enhance pathologist's usability to gain more pathology data and make more accurate deep learning model in image segmentation.
I received B.S. and M.S. degrees in the computer science department at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST). I worked with Sungahn Ko in the Interactive Visual Analysis and Data Exploration Research (iVADER) Lab.
My resesarch topics are visual analytics science and technology and human-computer interaction to be specific making interactive tools and data-driven applications with artificial intelligence techniques.
I have passion for UI effects, animations and creating intuitive, dynamic user experiences.
